Health Insurance Schemes

Aam Aadmi Bima Yojana

Aam Aadmi Bima Yojana (AABY) has been launched for those who work in various labors. Such as handloom weaving, fishing, carpentry, and other labor workers etc. This scheme is launched for these kinds of workers.

Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana

Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY) is an insurance scheme offering life insurance cover for death due to any reason. It is a one-year cover, renewable from year to year.
Know more...

Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana

An Accident Insurance Scheme offering accidental death and disability cover for death or disability on account of an accident.
Know more...

Chief Minister's Comprehensive Health Insurance Scheme

This is a state government scheme. It is promoted by the Tamil Nadu Government in association with United India Insurance Company Ltd. The Chief Minister’s Comprehensive Insurance Scheme is a family floater plan designed for quality health care.

Health Schemes For Pregnant Womens

Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana

The Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY) is a Centrally Sponsored flagship scheme by the Ministry of Women and Child Development, Government of India, designed to support pregnant women and lactating mothers.
Know more...

Surakshit Matritva Aashwasan Yojana

SUMAN Yojana or Surakshit Matritva Aashwasan Yojana is a maternity benefit initiative launched by the Ministry of Union Health and Family Welfare. This program provides affordable and quality healthcare solutions to pregnant women and newborns.
Know more...

Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana

A National Mission for Financial Inclusion (NMFI), namely, Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) was launched across the Country in August, 2014 by the Prime Minister with the idea to ensure that citizens envisage their financial activities.
Know more...

Janani Suraksha Yojana

The Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY) is a Govt of India sponsored Scheme which is being implemented with the objective of reducing maternal and infant mortality by promoting institutional delivery among pregnant women.
Know more...

Janani Shishu Suraksha Karyakram

A scheme by Ministry of MoHFW for pregnant women who access government health facilities for their delivery. to absolutely free and no expense delivery, including caesarean section. Essential care is provided to the mother and her neonate within 48 hours.
Know more...

Health Schemes

Ayushman Bharat Yojana

Ayushman Bharat Yojana was launched for those who are in a bad financial condition. Each household can receive medical coverage for major to extreme health issues.
Know more...

Health Minister’s Discretionary Grant

A health scheme by Ministry of Health & Family Welfare for financially poor patients to defray a part of the expenditure on Hospitalization/treatment in Government Hospitals,for life threatening diseases covered under Rashtriya Arogya Nidhi (RAN), in cases where free medical facilities are not available.
