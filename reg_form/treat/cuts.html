Step 1. Stop Bleeding - Press on the cut with a clean cloth.

Step 2. Clean It - Rinse with water to remove dirt.

Step 3. Apply Antiseptic - If you have it, put some on.

Step 4. Cover It - Use a bandage to keep it clean.

Note-


  • • Check Tetanus: If it's been a while, consider a shot.
  • • Watch for Infection: Look for redness or pus.
  • • Change Bandage: Keep it clean by changing it.
  • • Be Careful: Avoid rough activities that could hurt it more.
  • • Ask for Help: If you're not sure, get medical help.
  • • Avoid Certain Activities: Avoid activities that may reopen or aggravate the wound, such as heavy lifting or swimming
