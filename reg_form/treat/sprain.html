Based on the symptoms you've provided, the first aid treatment is as follows:

Step 1. Rest - Stop whatever you're doing and give the injured area a break.

Step 2. Ice - Put ice on it! Wrap ice in a cloth and hold it on the injury for 15-20 minutes, a few times a day.

Step 3. Wrap it up - Use an elastic bandage to wrap the injury gently to reduce swelling.

Step 4. Elevate - Raise the injured area above the heart to help reduce swelling.

Note-


  • • Pain relief: Take painkillers like ibuprofen or acetaminophen if needed, but follow the instructions.
  • • Get help: If it's really bad, see a doctor ASAP.
  • Remember, these steps can help a lot, but always trust your instincts and seek medical help if you're unsure or if the pain is severe.
  • If ice and bandage not available :
  • • Compression: If you have a clean cloth or clothing, you can use it to wrap the injured area gently for compression.
  • • Immobilization: If possible, try to keep the injured area still and avoid moving it unnecessarily to prevent further injury.
